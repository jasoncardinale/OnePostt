What is OnePostt?

Running a business is a very difficult task. While social media can be an amazing platform to promote your company, managing several accounts on a wide variety of sites can be a burden. The goal of OnePostt is to make managing all of your social media accounts as easy as possible.

How does it work?

After making an account with us you are able to connect to all of the most popular social media platforms directly through OnePostt. All of these profiles will be visible within your dashboard. Whether you have big news to report or simply want to stay up date with your followers, with OnePostt, you only need to make a single post.

Why does this help?

While most social media platforms are great means of spreading information, they each are unique; often making it necessary to significantly alter your original post to be compatible with a particular service. Below are just some of the changes that make promoting your business on social media insufficient and time-consuming.

Post Length

One of the most clear differences among the many social media platforms used today is the length of the post. For example, a post on a Facebook wall can often take up several paragraphs while a tweet must remain under 280 characters.

Images

Images are wonderful way to express your content in the world of social media. However, not every platform treats images equally. On sites such as Instagram, images make up for majority of a post's content as opposed to Twitter where there is a limited amount to visual content you can post.

Videos

Similar to images, videos tend to be treated differently on Instagram, Snapchat, and Pinterest, platforms that focus heavily on the posting of visual content, as opposed to Facebook, Twitter, and Reddit where videos behave more as links.

Polls

Some platforms such as Facebook and Twitter allow users to make polls that other users can interact with and record their responses. OnePostt will has a poll tool that allows you to create a poll in a simple fashion and post it to multiple social media platforms without having to rewrite anything.

Tags, Links, and Hashtags

A crucial component to social media is adding tags to posts that show the general topics that the content pertains to. While these take many forms across different platforms, OnePostt is able to recognize specific tags you put on your post and include them in the appropriate platforms.
